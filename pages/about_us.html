About Us

Driven by her pasion, Helen Moore traveled the world to broaden her knowledge about coffee and was eager to share it.
More than a decade ago she founded Soulsteem Coffee which its main goal is to deliver top tier beans from all around the globe within the extent of a click.
Since then, our search for the best and rarest roasts has not stopped and we keep working to discover and bring forth exceptional flavours to your home.
Now shipping to more than 30 countries and counting! We hope you enjoy our products as much as we do providing them. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
